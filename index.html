M.W HIGH SCHOOL


My School Name is M.W.(MERCHANT WORKERS)HIGH SCHOOL,Komdomtoly, Adamjeenagar, Narayangonj, Sadar. This School was built on 01 January, 1963. It is the one famous school in Narayangonj. This is developed by the merchant's of the Narayangonj in 19th century. This school holds the history of the war between Pakistani army's, the rular's and Bangladesh. there are around 5000 students in this school. M.W. HIGH SCHOOL is under registered to DHAKA EDUCATIONAL BOARD. There 3 types of streams offerred in this school these are: 1.Science, 2.Arts and 3. Commerce
